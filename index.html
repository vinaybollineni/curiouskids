About Us

A Home Away
From Home

Welcome to Curious Kids Learning Center LLC, a Texas Listed Family Home daycare operated by Meghana Vantipalli in Liberty Hill, TX. We believe the earliest years of childhood are the most important — and that small, personalized care makes all the difference.

By intentionally limiting enrollment to a maximum of 3 children, we ensure every child receives the individualized attention, warmth, and stimulation they need to grow and thrive — socially, emotionally, physically, and intellectually.
